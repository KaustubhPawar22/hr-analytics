## Overview
This project simulates a real-world HR analytics use case by generating a synthetic employee dataset and building an interactive Tableau dashboard to analyze workforce trends, demographics, and compensation patterns.

The goal is to provide HR stakeholders with both high-level summaries and detailed employee records to support data-driven decisions.

---

## Business Problem
Organizations often lack centralized visibility into employee demographics, hiring trends, and salary distribution. This dashboard enables HR managers to:

- Track hiring and termination trends
- Analyze workforce composition
- Compare salaries across gender, education, and departments
- Drill down into individual employee records

---

## Dataset
Synthetic dataset of 8,950 employees generated using Python.

Features include:

- Employee demographics
- Department and job title
- Education level
- Performance rating
- Salary and adjusted salary
- Hire and termination dates
- Location (state and city)

## Key Insights
- Higher attrition observed among early-tenure employees
- Salary varies significantly across departments and education levels
- Education level shows correlation with performance ratings
- Gender-based salary differences appear in specific roles

---

## Business Recommendations
- Implement targeted retention programs for early-career employees
- Monitor department-level attrition metrics regularly
- Standardize compensation bands
- Use performance insights to guide learning & development initiatives
